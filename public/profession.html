The Viola

I play a weird instrument. It ISN'T a violin, but it looks very similar! The differences between the viola and violin are:

  1. The viola is bigger.
  2. The violin's strings, from lowest to highest, are G, D, A, and E. The viola's strings are C, G, D, and A. So, the range is the same size but a little bit lower in pitch.
  3. Violists read a different clef in their music. It's called the alto clef.
